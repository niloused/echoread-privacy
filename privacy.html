Privacy Policy — Word2Talk

Last Updated: January 16, 2026

1. Introduction

Word2Talk ("we", "our", or "us") is dedicated to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use our Chrome Extension.

2. Information We Collect

The Word2Talk extension is designed to convert web articles into speech. To perform this function, the extension collects the following data:

  1. URL and Content of the Active Tab: When you explicitly click the "Play" button, the extension sends the URL and the text content of the current active tab to our backend server for processing.
  2. User Preferences: We store local preferences (such as playback speed, target language, and voice settings) locally on your device within the browser's storage.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Text-to-Speech Processing: The URL and text content are sent to our servers solely to extract the main article text and convert it into audio.
  • No Permanent Storage: We do not store the text or audio content of the articles you listen to after the session is complete. The data is processed in ephemeral sessions.
  • Improvement: We do not use your data for advertising or tracking purposes.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the data transmitted to our servers. Communication between the extension and our backend is encrypted using HTTPS.

5. Third-Party Services

Our backend processing may utilize third-party cloud providers (e.g., AWS) for hosting and computation. These providers process data in accordance with their own security standards.

6. Your Choices

You can choose not to use the extension on specific pages. The extension only activates and sends data when you explicitly interact with it.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@word2talk.com.
